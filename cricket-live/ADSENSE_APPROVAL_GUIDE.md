# Google AdSense Approval Guide - Fixing "Low Value Content"

## Current Issue
Your site shows "Needs attention" with "Low value content" status. This is common for new sites or those with primarily aggregated content.

## Why This Happens
1. **Thin Content**: Pages with minimal original text (< 300 words)
2. **Duplicate/Aggregated Content**: Mostly data from APIs without unique value
3. **Similar Pages**: Many pages that look identical in structure
4. **Lack of Original Content**: No unique editorial voice or insights

## Immediate Action Plan

### 1. Add Substantial Original Content (Priority: HIGH)

Each major page needs 500-1000 words of unique, valuable content:

#### Pages to Enhance:
- ✅ About page (DONE - expanded to ~800 words)
- Home page (add more editorial content below the fold)
- Tournament pages (IPL, T20 World Cup, etc.)
- Format pages (Test, ODI, T20)

## Content Writing Guidelines

### Quality Standards:
1. **Minimum 500 words** per major page
2. **Original writing** - no copying from other sites
3. **Proper grammar** and spelling
4. **Structured content** with headings and paragraphs
5. **Value-added** - teach, inform, or entertain

### Writing Style:
- Write for cricket fans, not just data seekers
- Add personality and voice
- Include anecdotes and stories
- Explain context, not just facts
- Use conversational tone

### SEO Best Practices:
- Use target keywords naturally
- Include related keywords
- Add internal links
- Use descriptive headings (H2, H3)
- Include images with alt text

❌ Spinning/rewriting existing articles

❌ Having broken links or errors
❌ Poor mobile experience
